Gaur (Indian Bison)

The Gaur (Bos gaurus), also known as the Indian Bison, is the largest wild cattle species in the world. It is mainly found in forested regions of India, especially in the Western Ghats.

Gaurs have a massive body, curved horns, and white markings on their legs. They play an important role in maintaining forest ecosystems.
