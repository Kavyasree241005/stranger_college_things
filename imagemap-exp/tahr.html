Nilgiri Tahr

The Nilgiri Tahr (Nilgiritragus hylocrius) is an endangered mountain goat species endemic to the Western Ghats of India.

It inhabits high-altitude grasslands and rocky cliffs, mainly in the Nilgiri and Anamalai hill ranges. The Nilgiri Tahr is known for its curved horns and strong climbing ability.
