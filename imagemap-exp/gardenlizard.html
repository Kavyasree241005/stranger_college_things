Oriental Garden Lizard

The Oriental Garden Lizard (Calotes versicolor), also known as the Changeable Lizard, is a common reptile found throughout India.

It is commonly seen in gardens, forest edges, and urban areas. The lizard has rough, spiny scales and can change its body color depending on mood and environmental conditions.
